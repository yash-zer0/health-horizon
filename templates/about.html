About Us

We are Aditya Karwani, Tanmay Mishra, and Yash Shrivastav, fourth-year B.Tech students specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies. Our passion for AI and machine learning drives us to innovate and create impactful solutions that enhance everyday lives.

Our latest project, Health Horizon, is designed to empower individuals in managing their health more effectively. By simply inputting symptoms, users can receive insights into potential diseases, along with detailed descriptions, precautions, recommended medications, workout plans, and dietary suggestions.

We believe that access to accurate health information is crucial, and through Health Horizon, we aim to provide a reliable resource that promotes informed decision-making and proactive health management.

As we continue our journey in the tech industry, we are committed to harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to make a positive difference in the world. Thank you for being part of our mission!
